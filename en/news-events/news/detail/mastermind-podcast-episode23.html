Mastermind-Podcast Episode#23

03/23/2022

In episode 23, Clarisse Schröder is a guest on the mastermind-podcast. She talks with Helen about the role of civil society and civic tech in an Open Commune, practical examples of how a municipality can support civic tech, and the benefits of openness for society as a whole.

(Kopie 4)

Clarisse is chairwoman of the association "Offene Kommunen.NRW Institut", in short OK NRW. The association is committed to openness, cooperation and transparency at the state and municipal level in NRW. In this context, they both talk about challenges, obstacles and fears when it comes to the topic of openness, as well as the Online Access Act, which stipulates that all administrative services must be digitized by the end of the current year.

In the latest episode of the mastermind-podcast, you can find out how realistic Clarisse Schröder thinks this goal is.
