Open Innovation Summer Party

09/05/2022

Summer is the best time to party - and preferably with nice people!

And how could we have celebrated this better than with you - the Open Innovation City Community. We were very pleased that so many people took the opportunity to get to know each other. After all, some actually knew each other "only" from the digital world!

We are sure that with so much drive and enthusiasm for the common cause, the community will continue to grow in the coming months and years. And we are happy to be part of this process!
