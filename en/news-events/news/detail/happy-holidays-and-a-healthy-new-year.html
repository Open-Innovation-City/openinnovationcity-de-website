Happy holidays and a healthy new year!

12/19/2022

An exciting, thrilling and eventful year 2022 lies behind us! A year in which we have achieved a lot together for an open culture of innovation in our city.

Still a bit slowed down by Corona, some great events and topics have been implemented nevertheless, which we proudly look back on. These include the numerous Talking about Innovation events we've hosted at Founders Foundation, all of which are watchable on YouTube. But also the many topic-related meetups in the Innovation Office, the now 40 podcast episodes or the Innovation Gym, with which we test the emergence of innovation processes with you.

Other highlights were certainly the BIE green! - City Hack and the knowledge transfers to Tampere (Finland), Alkmaar (Netherlands) and Barcelona (Spain). This has shown us what is possible in the community!

From this, we are now drawing new strength for the upcoming year 2023 and we are already looking forward to tackling the next big topics of Open Innovation City Bielefeld together with you!

Be excited about what is to come, enjoy the pre-Christmas season and stay healthy!

Your Open Innovation City Team!
